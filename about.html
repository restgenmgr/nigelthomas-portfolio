🇮🇳 Early Life & Education

Born in Bangalore on January 27, 1952. I grew up in the Garden City and completed my schooling at St. Joseph's Indian High School, one of Bangalore's most respected institutions. Those formative years taught me discipline, resilience, and the value of hard work — lessons that would guide my entire career.

🇮🇳 The Beginning – Mumbai & Rahat Palace

After completing my education, I migrated to Mumbai, the city of dreams. I started my hospitality career at Rahat Palace, Worli, Mumbai. This is where I learned the basics of hotel operations, guest service, and working under pressure. Mumbai's fast-paced environment prepared me for the international stage.

🇦🇪🇮🇶 Middle East Years – Ramada, Meridien, and Baghdad

My journey then took me to the Middle East. I worked at the Ramada Hotel and Meridien Hotel, where I was exposed to international standards of luxury service. I also spent time in Baghdad, Iraq, at the Al Hambra Hotel, which was a unique and challenging experience. In total, I spent about 6 years in the Middle East, learning to manage diverse teams and adapt to different cultural expectations.

🇺🇸 Twenty+ Years at Sea – US Cruise Ships

My most significant professional chapter began when I joined US cruise ships. For over 20 years, I sailed across the Caribbean, Bahamas, and US coasts, managing multi-outlet F&B operations for premium brands. I led teams of 100+ crew members, oversaw revenues exceeding $250,000 per voyage, and acted as project leader for dry dock refurbishments and new builds. Life at sea taught me operational excellence, crisis management, and how to deliver consistent quality in a moving, multicultural environment.

🇺🇸 On Land in the USA

After my sea career, I spent several years working on land in the United States as a Restaurant Operations Manager, directing high-volume locations from pre-opening to full maturity. This phase deepened my understanding of American-style service, cost control, and team leadership.

🇮🇳 Returning to India – Bangalore, Hyderabad & Nandi Hills

After decades abroad, I returned to India. I have worked across Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Nandi Hills, bringing my global experience to the growing Indian hospitality sector. Whether it is a fast-paced corporate hotel in Bangalore, a culturally rich property in Hyderabad, or a serene nature-focused resort in Nandi Hills, I have adapted and thrived. My focus remains on operational efficiency, team building, and guest satisfaction.
